Our Story

Furniture That Keeps Up With How You Actually Live

Anabei was founded on a simple frustration: why does beautiful furniture fall apart the moment life happens? The brand pioneered a fully washable sofa system that doesn't ask you to choose between style and practicality — you get both. From young families to pet owners to design-conscious renters, Anabei serves people who want their home to look good and handle whatever comes next.

Operating across the United States as part of the CABA Design portfolio, Anabei ships direct to consumers — no showroom markups, faster delivery, and more customization than traditional furniture retail. The result is a sofa that feels like a splurge but performs like a workhorse.
